About Our School

Nurturing Excellence Since 1992

Sengani Girls School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to young women. Established in 1992, we have a rich history of academic excellence and character development.

Our beautiful 25-acre campus provides the perfect environment for learning and personal growth. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we offer a holistic education that prepares students for success in university and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers young women to achieve academic excellence, develop strong character, and become ethical leaders who make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that nurtures innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and globally-minded citizens who are prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century with wisdom and integrity.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive Academic Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. We follow the national curriculum while enhancing it with our unique programs:

Core Subjects

  • Mathematics & Advanced Mathematics
  • English Language & Literature
  • Kiswahili Language & Literature
  • Integrated Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Humanities (History, Geography, Social Studies)
  • Religious Education

Specialized Programs

  • STEM Innovation Program
  • Performing & Visual Arts
  • Entrepreneurship & Business Studies
  • Information & Communication Technology
  • Languages Program (French, German, Mandarin)
  • Leadership & Life Skills Development

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

Our STEM program prepares students for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics with modern laboratories and research opportunities.

Program Highlights

  • State-of-the-art science laboratories
  • Robotics and coding programs
  • Annual science fair and innovation challenges
  • Partnerships with universities and tech companies
  • Research projects and mentorship programs
  • Advanced mathematics and physics programs

Facilities

  • Fully equipped physics, chemistry, biology labs
  • Computer lab with 3D printing capabilities
  • Robotics workshop
  • Green energy research station
  • Mathematics resource center
  • STEM innovation hub

Arts & Humanities Program

Explore creativity and critical thinking through our comprehensive arts program including literature, history, visual arts, and performing arts.

Program Highlights

  • Visual arts studio with professional equipment
  • Performing arts theater and dance studio
  • Creative writing and poetry workshops
  • Music program with various instrument options
  • Annual cultural festival and art exhibition
  • Debate and public speaking competitions

Facilities

  • 500-seat auditorium with professional lighting
  • Art studio with pottery kiln and printing press
  • Music rooms with various instruments
  • Drama studio with stage and costume department
  • Digital media lab for film and photography
  • Literature and languages resource center

Sports & Athletics Program

Develop physical fitness and teamwork through our diverse sports program including basketball, volleyball, track and field, and more.

Program Highlights

  • Competitive sports teams in 12 different disciplines
  • Annual inter-house sports championship
  • Fitness and wellness program
  • Sports science and nutrition education
  • Leadership training through team sports
  • Partnerships with national sports organizations

Facilities

  • Olympic-size swimming pool
  • Track and field stadium
  • Indoor sports complex (basketball, volleyball)
  • Tennis and netball courts
  • Fitness center with modern equipment
  • Sports medicine clinic
